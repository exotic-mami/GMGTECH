GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)

GEO is the practice of optimizing your brand, content, and digital presence so AI systems like ChatGPT, Google SGE, and Perplexity choose your business when generating answers. Unlike traditional SEO, GEO focuses on becoming the answer—not just ranking in search results.



1. Entity Clarity (Foundation)

This ensures AI can clearly identify, categorize, and trust your business.

  • Clear business name, category, and location
  • Consistent name, address, and phone (NAP)
  • Strong About pages written in plain language
  • Structured data (Schema: Organization, LocalBusiness, Service)

2. Answer-First Content

AI favors content that directly answers questions without fluff.

  • Headings written as direct questions
  • Short, factual paragraphs (40–80 words)
  • Lists, steps, and comparisons
  • Clear definitions early in the content

3. Topical Authority Clusters

Authority is built by covering a topic deeply, not lightly.

  • Core service pages
  • Supporting subtopic content
  • FAQs and comparisons
  • Depth over breadth

4. Proof & Credibility Signals

AI is risk-averse and prioritizes trustworthy businesses.

  • Google and third-party reviews
  • Case studies and real examples
  • Photos, videos, and credentials
  • Named authors or business owners

5. Brand Mentions (Off-Site Fuel)

AI learns about your brand from across the web, not just your website.

  • Local citations
  • Press and blog mentions
  • Forums, Reddit, and Quora
  • YouTube descriptions and transcripts

6. Comparison & Recommendation Triggers

These help AI choose your business when multiple options exist.

  • Best-of lists
  • Service comparisons (X vs Y)
  • Pros and cons
  • Who each option is best for

7. Freshness & Activity

Active, updated businesses are cited more frequently by AI.

  • Updated website content
  • New reviews
  • Active Google Business Profile
  • Ongoing publishing
