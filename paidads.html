Paid Ads



1. Goals & Strategy Setup

Before running any ads, the campaign goals and overall strategy must be clearly defined to ensure ad spend is tied to real business outcomes.

  • Define primary objective (leads, sales, calls, traffic, awareness)
  • Set success metrics (CPA, ROAS, CTR, conversion rate)
  • Identify target audience (location, age, interests, behaviors)
  • Choose ad platform(s)
  • Determine budget and campaign duration
  • Align ads with current offers or promotions

    • 2. Tracking & Analytics

    Tracking is essential to measure performance and optimize results. Without it, ad spend cannot be justified or improved.

    • Install conversion tracking (Pixel, Tag, GA4)
    • Set up custom conversions and events
    • Verify tracking is firing correctly
    • Connect CRM or lead forms if applicable
    • Enable call tracking if running call ads
    • Set up UTM parameters for reporting

    3. Audience Targeting

    Well-defined targeting ensures ads reach the right people and reduces wasted spend.

    • Create core audience targeting
    • Build custom audiences
    • Set up lookalike audiences
    • Exclude irrelevant or low-quality audiences
    • Segment audiences by intent or funnel stage

    4. Ad Creative & Copy

    Strong creative and messaging directly impact click-through rates and conversions.

    • Write clear, benefit-driven headlines
    • Create compelling primary ad copy
    • Design high-quality images or videos
    • Ensure creative matches brand guidelines
    • Include strong calls-to-action
    • Create multiple variations for testing

    5. Landing Page Optimization

    Ads are only as effective as the page they send traffic to. Landing pages should be fast, focused, and conversion-driven.

    • Match landing page messaging to ad copy
    • Ensure page loads quickly on mobile and desktop
    • Use clear headlines and value propositions
    • Include visible CTAs and contact methods
    • Remove unnecessary distractions
    • Add trust signals such as reviews or testimonials

    6. Optimization & Scaling

    Ongoing optimization ensures consistent performance and improved results over time.

    • Monitor performance regularly
    • Pause underperforming ads and audiences
    • Scale winning ads and creatives
    • Adjust budgets based on performance
    • Refresh creatives to avoid ad fatigue
    • Test new audiences, offers, and formats

    7. Reporting & Communication

    Clear reporting builds trust and shows the value of paid advertising.

    • Track KPIs consistently
    • Prepare weekly or monthly performance reports
    • Highlight wins, insights, and improvements
    • Communicate next steps and recommendations
    • Align results with original campaign goals
